FreshRoots

Agriculture & Food Processing

Venture code: VEN-FRES-8057

The startup aims to address key challenges in the food supply chain by empowering farmers and providing consumers with access to fresh, healthy produce. One major issue is the unfair pricing faced by farmers due to the involvement of numerous middlemen who dictate the rates at which crops are bought and sold. To solve this, the startup creates a direct-to-consumer (D2C) platform that enables farmers to bypass intermediaries and sell their produce directly to consumers, allowing them to secure better prices and reduce costs.

In urban areas, many people struggle to access fresh, unadulterated food due to preservatives and long supply chains. The startup resolves this by offering consumers a platform that connects them directly to farmers, ensuring healthier, fresher food options. Furthermore, a lack of transparency in the food supply chain affects all stakeholders, from farmers to retailers and consumers. To tackle this, the startup offers a mobile application that tracks and provides transparency about the origin and production process of food, fostering trust and sustainability in the supply chain.
